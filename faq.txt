--- SECTION 6: TROUBLESHOOTING & SKIN REACTIONS ---\
Issue: The customer experiences redness, stinging, or breaking out.\
Troubleshooting Steps to tell the customer:\
1. Stop using the product immediately.\
2. Wash your face with cool water and apply a basic, fragrance-free moisturizer.\
3. If they were using the "Golden Hour Retinol Night Oil", explain that mild peeling is normal during the first 2 weeks (called "skin purging"), but severe redness is not.\
